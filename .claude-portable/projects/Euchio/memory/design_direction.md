## Decision
User chose **方案A: Industrial Dark Style** over other design directions.

## Design Tokens
- Primary: `#dc2626` (red), light: `#ef4444`, dark: `#b91c1c`
- Background dark: `#0f172a`, alt: `#1e293b`
- Accent: `#f59e0b` (amber)
- Border radius: 4px (small), 6px (large) — minimal, industrial
- Font: Inter

## Section Layout
- Alternating dark/light sections
- Dark sections: Hero, How It Works, Cutting Samples, CTA
- Light sections: Advantages, Parameters, Reference Customers

## What Was Removed
- All emoji icons (replaced with text/numbers)
- 3-column card grids (replaced with bordered cards + colored left borders)
- Rounded corners (reduced to 4px)
- Decorative shadows (replaced with borders)
- Trust bar (user rejected: "没有任何设计，你还不如不放呢")

## Design Review Score
- Before: 5/10
- After (projected): ~7/10
- Deferred: DESIGN.md creation, advantages layout variants
