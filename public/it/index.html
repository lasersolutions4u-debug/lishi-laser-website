Come Funziona

Cos'è un Dispositivo a Gas Misto?

Il nostro dispositivo di miscelazione azoto-ossigeno utilizza tecnologia di taglio laser a micro-ossigeno to produce a precisely calibrated N₂/O₂ mixed gas — eliminating burrs on carbon steel while delivering 3× faster cutting speed than traditional oxygen cutting. Designed for high power laser machines from 3kW to 60kW.

Competitive Edge

Perché Scegliere Gas Misto invece di O₂, N₂ o Aria?

Burr-free edges, 3× faster cutting speed, and near-zero electricity cost. Our maintenance-free gas supply system is compatible with Han's laser, DNE, PENTA, LEAD, HSG, BODOR and other major brands.

Taglio 3× Più Veloce

Aumenta la velocità di taglio laser fino a 3× rispetto al taglio a ossigeno. Esempio: acciaio al carbonio 8mm a 16m/min con gas misto vs solo 2–3m/min con O₂.

Elimina le Bave

A differenza del taglio ad azoto che produce bave su lamiere ≥8mm (12kW) o ≥10mm (20kW), il taglio a gas misto produce superfici pulite e senza bave su acciaio al carbonio — nessuna molatura secondaria necessaria.

Ultra-Low Costo Energetico

Only 2 kWh per 24 hours — near-zero electricity cost. Unlike air compressors that consume 15–30 kW continuously, the mixed gas device operates with minimal power draw while delivering 3× faster cutting performance.

Esente da Manutenzione

Consumo energetico: solo 2 kWh / 24 ore. A differenza dei compressori d'aria che richiedono cambio filtri/olio ogni 3.000 ore, la nostra apparecchiatura gas laser industriale è esente da manutenzione.

One-to-Three Laser Setup

Stable one-to-three mixed gas equipment configuration. One industrial gas mixing station powers up to three laser machines of different power levels simultaneously.

Protezione Lenti

Aria compressors risk oil/water contamination burning laser head lenses ($5,000–$50,000 loss). Our pure liquid gas source keeps your optical system 100% safe.

FAQ

Domande Frequenti

Cos'è un dispositivo a gas misto e come funziona?

A mixed gas device precisely blends liquid nitrogen (N₂) and liquid oxygen (O₂) into a calibrated N₂/O₂ gas mixture (typically 95%/5%). This micro-oxygen mixture is used as the auxiliary gas in high-power laser cutting, delivering 3× faster cutting speed on carbon steel compared to pure oxygen, while eliminating burrs entirely.

È compatibile con la mia macchina laser?

Yes. The EUCHIO Mixed Gas Dispositivo a Gas Misto works with all major laser brands including HAN'S, DNE, PENTA, LEAD, HSG, BODOR, JIATAI, HG LASER, and XUNLEI. It supports machines from 3kW to 60kW. If your machine uses standard auxiliary gas connections, it is compatible.

Quali spessori può tagliare?

Cutting range depends on laser power: 12kW handles up to 16mm carbon steel, 20kW up to 25mm, 30kW up to 30mm, and 60kW up to 40mm. It also cuts stainless steel and aluminum with excellent results.

How much can I save on operating costs?

The mixed gas device consumes only 2 kWh per 24 hours — near-zero electricity cost. The primary savings come from 3× faster cutting speed (more parts per shift) and eliminating manual deburring labor. Gas costs may be similar or slightly higher than pure N₂, but the throughput and quality gains far outweigh this for professional laser cutting shops.

Il dispositivo richiede manutenzione regolare?

No. Unlike air compressors that need filter/oil changes every 500–3,000 hours, the EUCHIO Dispositivo a Gas Misto is maintenance-free. It uses pure liquid gas sources with no moving parts that wear out. Power consumption is just 2 kWh/24h.

Can one device serve multiple laser machines?

Yes. The EUCHIO Mixed Gas supports a stable One-to-Three configuration. One mixing station can simultaneously supply up to three laser machines of different power levels (e.g., one 12kW and one 20kW).

In cosa il gas misto è diverso dal taglio ad aria?

Aria cutting produces oxidized, rough edges and risks oil/water contamination that can burn expensive laser head lenses ($5,000–$50,000). Gas Misto delivers smooth, burr-free, silver-white cut surfaces with zero contamination risk — and cutting speeds 30% faster than air.

Quanto tempo richiede l'installazione?

Installation is straightforward and typically completed within one day. The device connects to your existing liquid gas supply and laser machine. We provide detailed installation guides and remote technical support for all customers.
