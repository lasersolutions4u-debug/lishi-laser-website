Our Company

Who We Are

Jinan Euchio Machinery Co., Ltd. is a China-based machinery trading and service company focused on sheet metal processing equipment and related industrial solutions. We work with manufacturers in China and connect their equipment with overseas industrial customers.

The company operates two brands: EUCHIO for complete machines that can be supplied under our own brand identity, and SAGEMRO for MRO products and services — including maintenance, repair, spare parts, and peripheral equipment. Products that cannot be rebranded, or that come from manufacturers who do not allow private labeling, are offered under SAGEMRO or through our online store.

Our goal is straightforward: provide practical, well-matched equipment and service solutions for overseas industrial customers, with clear communication and long-term support. We focus on being honest about what we can and cannot do — if a product isn't the right fit, we'll say so.

Product Development

Identified recurring auxiliary gas problems among laser cutting customers. Developed a mixed gas regulation device covering 3kW to 60kW fiber lasers. First units deployed at customer sites for field testing.

Field Verification

Launched gasmixtech.com to document what the device does and share field-test results. Verified cutting data collected from 12kW, 20kW, 30kW, 40kW, and 60kW customer deployments.

Global Reach

Multi-language website launched (15 languages). Continuing to add verified data from new customer sites. Sales supported through DHgate online store and direct contact.

Our Brands

Two Brands, One Mission

We operate two brands to cover different equipment and service needs — clearly separated so customers always know what they're getting.

EUCHIO — Complete Machines

EUCHIO is our brand for private-label complete machines, including laser cutting machines, sheet shearing machines, tube cutting machines, press brakes, and other sheet metal fabrication equipment. It is used when complete machines are supplied under our own brand identity.

Website: euchio.com

SAGEMRO — MRO Parts & Service

SAGEMRO focuses on MRO-related products and services — maintenance, repair, operation support, spare parts, wearing parts, equipment upgrades, retrofit solutions, and peripheral equipment for laser cutting, welding, bending, and other metalworking applications. Products not suitable for private labeling are also offered under SAGEMRO.

Website: sagemro.com

Where does the gas mixing device fit? The mixed gas device is a proprietary product that is not offered for private labeling. It is supplied under the SAGEMRO brand. For after-sales service, maintenance, and technical support, visit sagemro.com.

What We Do

Equipment, Parts, and Service — Done Right

Complete Machines

Through the EUCHIO brand, we supply laser cutting machines, press brakes, tube cutters, and shearing machines under our own brand identity — for customers who need turnkey equipment solutions.

MRO Parts & Service

Through SAGEMRO, we provide spare parts, wearing parts, equipment upgrades, retrofit solutions, and peripheral equipment for laser cutting, welding, bending, and other metalworking applications.

Gas Mixing Technology

Our proprietary nitrogen-oxygen gas mixing devices for laser cutting (3kW–60kW) are offered under the SAGEMRO brand. One device can supply up to three laser machines simultaneously, cutting 3x faster with zero burrs and 33% less nitrogen consumption.

Technical Consulting

We assess your laser power, material thickness, and gas supply setup before recommending a solution. If mixed gas isn't right for your application, we'll tell you.

After-Sales Support

SAGEMRO provides ongoing maintenance, repair, and operation support for all equipment we supply. Long-term support is not an add-on — it's built into how we work.

Responsible Sourcing

We select manufacturing partners carefully and communicate honestly about what each product can and cannot do. No inflated specs, no false promises.

Why Choose Us

Honest About What We Are — and Aren't

We Tell You If It Won't Work

Before quoting, we check whether mixed gas makes sense for your laser power, material, and gas supply. If it doesn't, we'll say so. We'd rather lose a sale than ship the wrong solution.

Real Data, Not Marketing Numbers

Our parameter tables distinguish between verified field data and estimated reference values. We label what's tested and what's extrapolated — so you can make decisions with honest numbers.

One Device, Three Machines

The one-to-three configuration lets a single gas mixing station supply up to three laser machines of different power levels. Fewer devices, less complexity, lower cost.

2 kWh per 24 Hours
