Wat is een Menggasapparaat?

Ons stikstof-zuurstof mengapparaat gebruikt micro-zuurstof lasersnijtechnologie to produce a precisely calibrated N₂/O₂ mixed gas — eliminating burrs on carbon steel while delivering 3× faster cutting speed than traditional oxygen cutting. Designed for high power laser machines from 3kW to 60kW.

Waarom Kiezen voor Menggas in Plaats van O₂, N₂ of Lucht?

Burr-free edges, 3× faster cutting speed, and near-zero electricity cost. Our maintenance-free gas supply system is compatible with Han's laser, DNE, PENTA, LEAD, HSG, BODOR and other major brands.

3× Sneller Snijden

Verhoog de lasersnijsnelheid tot 3× in vergelijking met zuurstofsnijden. Voorbeeld: 8mm koolstofstaal op 16m/min met menggas vs slechts 2–3m/min met O₂.

Elimineer Bramen

In tegenstelling tot stikstofsnijden dat bramen produceert op platen ≥8mm (12kW) of ≥10mm (20kW), produceert menggassnijden schone, braamvrije oppervlakken op koolstofstaal — geen secundair slijpen nodig.

Ultra-Low Stroomkosten

Only 2 kWh per 24 hours — near-zero electricity cost. Unlike air compressors that consume 15–30 kW continuously, the mixed gas device operates with minimal power draw while delivering 3× faster cutting performance.

Onderhoudsvrij

Stroomverbruik: slechts 2 kWh / 24 uur. In tegenstelling tot luchtcompressoren die elke 3.000 uur filter-/olieverversing nodig hebben, is onze industriële lasergasapparatuur onderhoudsvrij.

One-to-Three Laser Setup

Stable one-to-three mixed gas equipment configuration. One industrial gas mixing station powers up to three laser machines of different power levels simultaneously.

Lensbescherming

Lucht compressors risk oil/water contamination burning laser head lenses ($5,000–$50,000 loss). Our pure liquid gas source keeps your optical system 100% safe.

FAQ

Veelgestelde Vragen

Wat is een menggasapparaat en hoe werkt het?

A mixed gas device precisely blends liquid nitrogen (N₂) and liquid oxygen (O₂) into a calibrated N₂/O₂ gas mixture (typically 95%/5%). This micro-oxygen mixture is used as the auxiliary gas in high-power laser cutting, delivering 3× faster cutting speed on carbon steel compared to pure oxygen, while eliminating burrs entirely.

Is het compatibel met mijn lasermachine?

Yes. The EUCHIO Mixed Gas Menggasapparaat works with all major laser brands including HAN'S, DNE, PENTA, LEAD, HSG, BODOR, JIATAI, HG LASER, and XUNLEI. It supports machines from 3kW to 60kW. If your machine uses standard auxiliary gas connections, it is compatible.

Welke diktes kan het snijden?

Cutting range depends on laser power: 12kW handles up to 16mm carbon steel, 20kW up to 25mm, 30kW up to 30mm, and 60kW up to 40mm. It also cuts stainless steel and aluminum with excellent results.

How much can I save on operating costs?

The mixed gas device consumes only 2 kWh per 24 hours — near-zero electricity cost. The primary savings come from 3× faster cutting speed (more parts per shift) and eliminating manual deburring labor. Gas costs may be similar or slightly higher than pure N₂, but the throughput and quality gains far outweigh this for professional laser cutting shops.

Heeft het apparaat regelmatig onderhoud nodig?

No. Unlike air compressors that need filter/oil changes every 500–3,000 hours, the EUCHIO Menggasapparaat is maintenance-free. It uses pure liquid gas sources with no moving parts that wear out. Power consumption is just 2 kWh/24u.

Can one device serve multiple laser machines?

Yes. The EUCHIO Mixed Gas supports a stable One-to-Three configuration. One mixing station can simultaneously supply up to three laser machines of different power levels (e.g., one 12kW and one 20kW).

Hoe verschilt menggas van luchtsnijden?

Lucht cutting produces oxidized, rough edges and risks oil/water contamination that can burn expensive laser head lenses ($5,000–$50,000). Menggas delivers smooth, burr-free, silver-white cut surfaces with zero contamination risk — and cutting speeds 30% faster than air.

Hoe lang duurt de installatie?

Installation is straightforward and typically completed within one day. The device connects to your existing liquid gas supply and laser machine. We provide detailed installation guides and remote technical support for all customers.

Klaar om Uw Snijsnelheid te Verhogen?

Sluit één apparaat aan op uw lasermachine en begin vandaag nog 3× sneller te snijden. Beschikbaar voor machines van 6KW–60KW.
