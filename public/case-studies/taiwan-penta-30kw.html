The Challenge

A precision metal fabrication shop in Taiwan operates a PENTA 30kW fiber laser, cutting carbon steel plates from 8mm to 20mm thickness. Their customers demand welder-ready edges — no oxidation, no grinding, no secondary processing.

  • Laser: PENTA 30kW fiber laser
  • Material: Carbon steel (8mm–20mm)
  • Quality requirement: Zero burrs, weld-ready edges
  • Previous gas: Pure oxygen — slow speeds with heavy edge oxidation

The Solution: EUCHIO Mixed Gas — Single Station

EUCHIO Mixed Gas installed one mixed gas station feeding the PENTA 30kW laser. The system delivers a precise N₂/O₂ blend that enables high-temperature oxidation at the cutting front — combining oxygen's reactivity with nitrogen's cooling and edge protection.

The result: clean silver-white edges that go straight to welding, at speeds 3-5x faster than pure oxygen.

Key Takeaways

3-5x O₂ Speed

Mixed gas consistently delivers 3-5x the cutting speed of pure oxygen on the same machine. The 30kW laser performs like a much larger system on pure O₂.

Weld-Ready Edges

All thicknesses produced silver-white edges with zero oxidation. Parts go directly from the laser bed to welding — no grinding station needed.

PENTA Compatible

Plug-and-play with PENTA laser systems. No modifications to the laser — connects directly to existing gas inlets. One mixing station can feed two PENTA lasers simultaneously.
