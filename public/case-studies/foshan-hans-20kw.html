The Challenge

A high-volume sheet metal fabricator in Foshan runs two HAN'S 20kW fiber lasers 16 hours a day, processing carbon steel from 3mm to 16mm. With a growing order book and tight delivery deadlines, every minute of beam-on time counted.

  • Lasers: 2× HAN'S 20kW fiber lasers
  • Material: Carbon steel (3mm–16mm)
  • Operation: 16 hours/day, 6 days/week
  • Previous gas: Pure oxygen — speeds too slow to meet order volume
  • Bottleneck: Cutting speed was the limiting factor; downstream welding and assembly were waiting on parts

The Solution: EUCHIO Mixed Gas — One Station Feeding Two Lasers

EUCHIO Mixed Gas installed a single mixed gas station feeding both HAN'S 20kW lasers simultaneously. The precise N₂/O₂ blend enables high-temperature oxidation at the cut front — delivering oxygen's cutting energy with nitrogen's clean edge profile.

The shop saw immediate throughput gains: cutting speeds jumped 2.5–3.5x across all thicknesses. Daily output increased 160% without adding shifts or machines.
