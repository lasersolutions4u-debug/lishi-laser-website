Что такое устройство смешанного газа?

Our nitrogen oxygen mixing device uses микрокислородная технология лазерной резки to produce a precisely calibrated N₂/O₂ mixed gas — eliminating burrs on carbon steel while delivering 3× faster cutting speed than traditional oxygen cutting. Designed for high power laser machines from 12kW to 60kW.

Вопросы

Часто задаваемые вопросы

Что такое устройство смешанного газа и как оно работает?

A mixed gas device precisely blends liquid nitrogen (N₂) and liquid oxygen (O₂) into a calibrated N₂/O₂ gas mixture (typically 95%/5%). This micro-oxygen mixture is used as the auxiliary gas in high-power laser cutting, delivering 3× faster cutting speed on carbon steel compared to pure oxygen, while eliminating burrs entirely.

Совместимо ли с моим лазерным станком?

Yes. The LISHI LASER Устройство смешанного газа works with all major laser brands including HAN'S, DNE, PENTA, LEAD, HSG, BODOR, JIATAI, HG LASER, and XUNLEI. It supports machines from 12kW to 60kW. If your machine uses standard auxiliary gas connections, it is compatible.

What thickness can it cut?

Cutting range depends on laser power: 12kW handles up to 16mm carbon steel, 20kW up to 25mm, 30kW up to 30mm, and 60kW up to 40mm. It also cuts stainless steel and aluminum with excellent results.

How much can I save on gas costs?

The mixed gas device is designed for speed and cut quality — not gas savings. It consumes only 2 kWh per 24 hours (near-zero electricity cost). The primary value is 3× faster cutting and burr-free edges, which eliminate manual deburring labor. Gas costs may be similar or higher than pure N₂, but the throughput and quality gains far outweigh the difference for professional shops.

Does the device require regular maintenance?

No. Unlike air compressors that need filter/oil changes every 500–3,000 hours, the LISHI Устройство смешанного газа is maintenance-free. It uses pure liquid gas sources with no moving parts that wear out. Power consumption is just 2 kWh/24h.

Can one device serve two laser machines?

Yes. LISHI LASER is the only manufacturer offering a stable One-to-Two configuration. One mixing station can simultaneously supply two laser machines of different power levels (e.g., one 12kW and one 20kW).

How is mixed gas different from air cutting?

Air cutting produces oxidized, rough edges and risks oil/water contamination that can burn expensive laser head lenses ($5,000–$50,000). Mixed gas delivers smooth, burr-free, silver-white cut surfaces with zero contamination risk — and cutting speeds 30% faster than air.

How long does installation take?

Installation is straightforward and typically completed within one day. The device connects to your existing liquid gas supply and laser machine. We provide detailed installation guides and remote technical support for all customers.
