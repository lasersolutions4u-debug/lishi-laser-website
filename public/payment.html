Payment

T/T Bank Transfer for Confirmed Equipment Orders

We do not use online checkout for mixed gas device orders. Payment is arranged by bank transfer after technical confirmation and a formal proforma invoice.

Standard Order Flow

  • Inquiry and technical assessment based on your machine and cutting scenario.
  • Configuration confirmation, quotation, shipping discussion, and commercial terms.
  • Formal proforma invoice issued by Jinan Euchio Machinery Co., Ltd.
  • T/T deposit paid to the bank account shown on the confirmed PI.
  • Production, inspection, shipping preparation, and balance payment before shipment.

Bank Account Safety

  • Bank details are provided only on the formal PI or confirmed business email.
  • Do not send payment to any account changed by an informal email or chat message.
  • If you receive a bank account change notice, verify it by calling our official phone number.
  • Use the PI number, company name, and project reference in the bank transfer remarks when possible.

No Direct Online Payment

This page is not a checkout page. Please do not make any payment before receiving a confirmed PI from our sales team. Full bank account information is not published on the website for security reasons.

Need a PI?

Send your technical details first. We will confirm whether the project is suitable before issuing a quotation or PI.
