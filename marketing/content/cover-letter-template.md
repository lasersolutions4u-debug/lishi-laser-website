# 邮寄附信模板（Cover Letter）

> 使用说明: `[方括号]` 内容需根据收件人个性化替换。
> 打印在 A4 公司信纸（带 logo + 联系方式页眉）上。

---

## 使用场景适配

### 冷邮寄（无联系历史）
→ 使用完整模板。第一段保留"many companies face..."引子。

### LinkedIn 联系后的跟进邮寄
→ 第一段改为:
"We connected on LinkedIn last week. As promised, I'm sending over
detailed information about the Mixed Gas Device for your [X] kW setup."

### 展会/拜访后的跟进邮寄
→ 第一段改为:
"It was great meeting you at [Trade Show Name]. Following up on our
conversation about [specific topic discussed], here's the detailed
information."

### ThomasNet / DirectIndustry 询盘后的邮寄
→ 第一段改为:
"Thank you for your inquiry on [Platform Name]. We've put together
a package that addresses your [specific request]."
