03 / Competitive Edge

Why Job Shops Choose Gas Mixer

Beyond electricity savings, the gas mixer delivers measurable quality and productivity improvements that win bids.

⚡

3× Faster Than O₂ Cutting

Micro-oxygen (5% O₂ / 95% N₂) enables cutting speeds 3× faster than pure oxygen on carbon steel. More parts per shift, shorter lead times, happier customers.

✨

Burr-Free Edge Quality

No oxidation, no secondary finishing. Parts come off the laser ready to weld, paint, or ship. Eliminates grinding/deburring labor — typically 15–25% of total fab cost (industry estimate).

🔧

One Device, Three Lasers

A single gas mixer unit supplies up to 3 fiber laser cutting machines simultaneously. Scale your capacity without scaling your electricity bill.

🛡️

Near-Zero Maintenance

No filters to change monthly. No oil to top up. No belts to tension. No condensate drain issues. Just connect and run — the system self-regulates.

🌐

Universal Compatibility

Works with Trumpf, Bystronic, Amada, Mazak, LVD, Prima Power, HSG, Bodor, and all other major fiber laser brands. 12kW–60kW range covers the vast majority of high-power laser installations.

📦

Compact & Portable

At 800×350×1100mm and 90kg, it fits through any standard door. No special foundation, no crane needed. Install in under 2 hours — plug in N₂ bottle and power.

China Market Reference

Across China, there are now over 1,000 gas mixer users, concentrated in the Pearl River Delta and Yangtze River Delta manufacturing clusters. In Foshan (Guangdong) alone — a dense sheet metal hub within the PRD — hundreds of shops have adopted the technology. For these professional job shops, gas mixers are no longer optional: they are standard equipment. Shops without them cannot compete on speed, quality, or operating cost. Reference customers in Foshan are available for visits at any time. This is where Mexico is heading.
